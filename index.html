I hope you can forgive me for my lack of respect and boundary violations. I'm committed to doing better and being the kind of mentee who honors the investment you're making in our development.

Thank you for taking the time to read this letter. I appreciate your consideration, regardless of what you decide going forward.

Sincerely,
